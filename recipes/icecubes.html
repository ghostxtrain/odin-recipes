Bonus Recipe : Ice Cubes

Here we are, learning how to cook ice cubes. If you're feeling like a particularly bougie degenerate, this one is for you.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ice cube tray
  • Freezer

Directions

Take ice cube tray and hold it under the faucet.
Turn on the faucet and begin to fill the ice tray. If needed, refer to the cup of water recipe as it will refresh your memory about this process.
Continue running the faucet until the ice tray is full.
Once the ice tray is filled, turn off the faucet.
Place the ice cube tray in the freezer and let it marinate for around 6 hours.
After the ice cubes are frozen solid, you can now add them to a wide variety of drinks, such as cup of water or white russian.
Use your imagination! Put ice cubes in all drinks which you want to remain cold!
