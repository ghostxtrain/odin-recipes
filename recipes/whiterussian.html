Classic White Russian

This is the Degenerate Recipes version of a milkshake and it is absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Kahlua
  • Cream or Milk
  • "Lowball" or "rocks" glass ~ 6-8 oz
  • Ice

Directions

First things first, let's gather all of our ingredients for easy drink construction.
Now we will set up our rocks glass with a few ice cubes. Don't worry if you're unfamiliar with cooking ice cubes, we cover that here as a little bonus.
We are going to now add together our ingredients starting with vodka and kahlua. The recipe calls for:
  • 2 parts vodka
  • 1 part kahlua
  • 1 part cream
Pour 2 oz of vodka and 1 oz kahlua over ice into the glass.
Add 1 oz of cream, or milk, on top of the liquor and ice mixture.
Okay, so the groovy part is lightly stirring the drink to get a swirl effect, similar to the picture at the top of our page.
Ideally, for stirring, we should use a straw. If for some reason all of our straws are unavailable ;) just use a finger.
