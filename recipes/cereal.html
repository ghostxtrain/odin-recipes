THE B.O.C : Breakfast Of Champions

...Bowl Of Cereal

This dish may not be the breakfast of actual champions, however, this B.O.C is a staple of Degenrate Cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Standard Bowl
  • Spoon (ideally a clean spoon)

Directions

Pepare ingredients by organizing on a flat surface, such as a kitchen counter.
First, we will add the desired amount of cereal to our bowl.
Next, we will add our milk at a ratio of of 55% cereal to 45% milk. Feel free to experiment with a milk to cereal ratio which you find desirable.
Once we have determined our ideal milk to cereal ratio, it is time to add the spoon to the bowl.
With all of our ingredients forming the B.O.C, we are now free to migrate to the couch and binge watch cartoons while we eat.
Optional: Add sugar if your B.O.C needs a bit of a flavor kick or if you need that sweet sugar rush.


Congratulations! You have just cooked one of the many delicacies of Degenerate Cuisine and we hope you enjoy :)
